About the Quest

When a brilliant, if domestically challenged, wizard decides to pursue eternal life, his best friend—a painfully earnest cleric—gets roped into the kind of divine clerical error that has cosmic consequences.

Unlikely Friendship

What happens when a devout cleric and a chaos-loving wizard who smells faintly of burnt toast become best mates? Heartwarming, hilarious, and horrifying adventures, that's what.

Existential Dread, But Fun

Explore the cosmic mysteries of life, death, and undeath. You'll ponder your own mortality while wondering if a detached limb is a legitimate workplace injury.

A Grand Adventure

From biscuit-thieving imps in a leaning tower to the sanity-shattering void of Acheron's Veil, this quest has it all. Pack extra socks and a healthy dose of skepticism.

Meet the Scribe

The slightly caffeinated mind behind the madness, Matthew Bowen.

By day, Matthew Bowen is a middle-aged, bearded author who wrangles his own chaotic adventuring party of three young kids, all under the command of their raid leader: his amazing wife. He assures everyone that his beard, while impressive, is mostly for storing biscuit crumbs and does not, to his knowledge, contain any arcane spell components. By night, fueled by an unhealthy amount of coffee, he trades his dad-duties for lich-duties, exploring the kinds of questionable life choices one makes when faced with eternity. His extensive experience in fantasy realms like Azeroth has taught him two things: 1) grinding for epic loot is remarkably similar to grinding out a novel, and 2) you should never, ever trust a wizard who says his new experiment is "perfectly safe." He believes that even after transcending the petty squalor of mortal needs, you still need a friend to remind you where you left your notes, and a very, very patient spouse to remind you to take out the trash.
