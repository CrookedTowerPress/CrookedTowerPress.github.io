About the Misadventure

When Elias decides to solve the ultimate problem of mortality by becoming undead, Thaddeus's steadfast loyalty plunges him into a quest he is hilariously unprepared for. It's a journey that will test his faith, his friendships, and the structural integrity of his own limbs.

Unlikely Friendship

A cleric who believes in meticulous order. A wizard who uses priceless grimoires as coasters. Their friendship proves that even after transcending mortal needs, you still need someone to remind you where you left your notes.

Existential Dread, But Fun

Tackle the big questions: What is the price of eternal life? And, most importantly, if your own foot falls off in a ditch, do you simply put it back on and pretend nothing happened? A thoughtful and absurd exploration of what it means to be alive...ish.

A Grand Adventure

Join an unlikely party on a journey from a tower infested with biscuit-thieving imps to a horrifying orchard of living flesh and the sanity-shattering depths of Acheron's Veil itself. Pack extra socks. And snacks. The imps will thank you.

Meet the Scribe

The slightly caffeinated mind behind the madness, Matthew Bowen.

By day, Matthew wrangles a chaotic adventuring party of three young kids, all under the command of their raid leader: his awesome wife. He strives for the meticulous order of his cleric, Thaddeus, but his office usually resembles the chaotic, explosion-prone laboratory of his wizard, Elias.

By night, fueled by coffee, he trades dad-duties for lich-duties, exploring the questionable choices one makes when faced with eternity. He firmly believes that even after you become an all-powerful undead entity, you still need a friend to remind you where you left your notes.
